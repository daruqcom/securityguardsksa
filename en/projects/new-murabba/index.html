Security Guard Services for New Murabba

The Mukaab's construction is one of the most architecturally ambitious projects in Riyadh right now, and the construction-site security supporting it needs to match that ambition: precise, well-documented, and ready for the international attention this specific site draws.

A Landmark Under Construction, Under Constant Watch

Given the Mukaab's striking cube design, the site draws genuine public and media photography interest even mid-build, and our security team manages this appropriately, distinguishing legitimate media coordination arranged through proper channels from unauthorised attempts to access restricted areas for photos.

Coordinating Multiple Contractors on One Site

The Mukaab and its surrounding district involve several major contractors working simultaneously on different structural and finishing phases, and our security coordination gives project management one consolidated view across every active contractor rather than each firm managing its own isolated arrangement disconnected from the broader site.

Vertical-Access Security for a Genuinely Unusual Structure

The Mukaab's distinctive vertical architecture brings access-control considerations specific to tall, unconventional structures: elevator and vertical-transport security, upper-floor access restrictions, and coordination with the building's own life-safety systems, all calibrated to a structure genuinely unlike a conventional office tower.

An International Contractor Workforce

A project of this ambition draws genuinely international engineering and construction teams, and our gate teams are briefed on functional English communication alongside Arabic, since a meaningful share of the on-site workforce isn't Arabic-speaking day to day.

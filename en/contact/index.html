Contact Us

Get a quote for security guard services anywhere in Saudi Arabia. Message us on WhatsApp with your location and site type, and we'll follow up with staffing options and pricing.

The fastest way to reach us is WhatsApp: tell us your city, the type of site (office, retail, residential, industrial, event), and whether you need coverage starting immediately or on a future date, and we'll respond with a tailored quote.

For anything that isn't urgent, or if you'd rather put a request in writing, reach us at contact@securityguardsksa.com. You can also follow our day-to-day work across Saudi Arabia on YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest.
